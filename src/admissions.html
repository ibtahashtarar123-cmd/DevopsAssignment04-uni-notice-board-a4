Eligibility & Requirements

Domestic Students

  • High School Diploma or equivalent (Undergraduates)
  • Bachelor's degree (Postgraduates)
  • Minimum GPA as per programme
  • Valid Government ID

International Students

  • Passport and visa eligibility
  • English Language Proficiency Test (TOEFL/IELTS)
  • Academic credentials evaluation
  • Proof of financial capacity
  • Health insurance coverage

Application Process

  1. Create Account

    Register on the online application portal with your email and personal details.

  2. Choose Programme

    Select your preferred programme and intake year from the available options.

  3. Submit Documents

    Upload transcripts, essays, references, and other supporting documents.

  4. Pay Application Fee

    Complete the non-refundable application fee (Fee waivers may be available).

  5. Interview/Assessment

    Selected candidates will be invited for interviews or entrance assessments.

  6. Receive Decision

    Admissions decision will be communicated via email and portal.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I apply to multiple programmes?

Yes, you can apply to up to three programmes in a single application cycle.

What is the application fee?

The application fee is $50 USD for all programmes. Some exceptions apply for low-income applicants.

How long does the admissions decision take?

Decisions are typically made within 4-6 weeks of the application deadline.

Do you offer scholarships?

Yes, merit-based and need-based scholarships are available. Apply through the scholarship portal.
